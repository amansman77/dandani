Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 13, 2025

Dandani ("we", "us", "our") is an emotional resilience practice app that helps users build healthier habits through small daily challenges.

We care deeply about your privacy. This document explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

1. Information We Collect

We designed Dandani to work with minimal personal data.

We do NOT collect:
  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contact list
  • Photos, media, or files (unless you explicitly share them through another channel)

1.1. Anonymous Usage Data

We may process the following information:

  • Anonymous device identifiers (e.g., a randomly generated ID)
  • App events (e.g., challenge completed, practice recorded)
  • Basic technical information (e.g., app version, OS version)

We use this only to understand how the app is used and to improve the service.

1.2. Practice Records and Notes

Your daily practice records may include:

  • Which challenge you completed
  • Your optional text notes about how you felt
  • Whether the practice was helpful

These records are:

  • Stored with an anonymous ID (no real name or email)
  • Used to show your own history and progress
  • Used in aggregate form to improve the service (e.g., which challenges are most helpful)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide core features (daily challenges, history, calendar)
  • Improve the app experience
  • Monitor app stability and performance
  • Analyze anonymized usage patterns and retention metrics
We DO NOT:
  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for targeted advertising

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • Data is stored on secure servers and/or local storage depending on the feature.
  • We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy.
  • Anonymous analytics data may be retained longer for statistical purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Dandani may use third-party services (e.g., analytics, error reporting, AI chat backend).
These services may receive anonymized data such as:

  • Anonymous user ID
  • Event types (e.g., "challenge_completed")
  • Basic device information

We choose third-party providers that comply with industry-standard security and privacy practices.

5. Children's Privacy

Dandani is intended for adults.
If you are under 16, please use the app only with the consent and supervision of a parent or legal guardian.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to certain types of processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:
