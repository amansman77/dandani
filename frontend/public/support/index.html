Dandani App Support

Last updated: January 28, 2026

Welcome to the support page for Dandani, the daily emotional resilience practice app. If you run into issues or have questions, use the contact options below.

Contact

Email: support@yetimates.com

Response time: within 2 business days

Languages: Korean, English

What to include in your request

  • Your device model and OS version (e.g., iPhone 15, iOS 18.2)
  • App version (Settings → About in the app)
  • A brief description of the issue and when it occurred
  • Screenshots if possible (no sensitive personal data please)

Common topics

  • Account & data: how anonymous IDs work, data deletion requests
  • Challenges & progress: resetting a challenge, tracking history
  • AI chat: conversation issues or inappropriate responses
  • Notifications: reminder delivery and scheduling
  • Billing (if applicable after launch): subscription issues and refunds

Data requests

To request deletion or export of your data, email us with the subject line “Dandani Data Request” and include your anonymous user ID from the app settings screen.

Outages & status

If the app is not loading or challenges are missing, check our status updates on X (Twitter).
