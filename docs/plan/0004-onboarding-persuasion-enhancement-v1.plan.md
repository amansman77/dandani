# 📘 plan_addendum.md — Onboarding Persuasion Enhancement (v1)

## 🎯 Objective

Improve onboarding conversion by solving:

> "Why should I use this?"

---

## 🔍 Current Issue

Even after refactoring:

* Structure is correct (What / How / Outcome)
* Actions are clear

BUT:

* No strong **personal trigger**
* No **urgency or self-recognition**
* Feels optional → low motivation to start

---

## 🧠 Core Problem

Current onboarding is:

* Informative ✔
* Structured ✔
* Actionable ✔

But NOT:

* Personally relevant ❌
* Emotionally activating ❌

---

## 🎯 Solution Strategy

Inject **self-recognition trigger** at the very first screen.

---

# 1️⃣ STEP 1 — Rewrite (CRITICAL)

## ❌ Current

```text
하루 1분, 내 감정을 정리하는 시간
```

---

## ✅ Replace With

### Version A (recommended)

```text
감정이 쉽게 흔들리는 날이 많다면
```

```text
하루 1분으로
내 중심을 다시 잡아보세요
```

```text
단단이는
작은 실천으로 나를 돌아보는 서비스입니다
```

---

## Why

* Converts abstract message → personal situation
* Triggers: "This might be for me"
* Creates entry motivation

---

# 2️⃣ CTA Optimization

## ❌ Current

```text
지금 시작하기
```

---

## ✅ Replace With

### Option A (recommended)

```text
지금 1분만 해보기
```

### Option B

```text
오늘 해보기
```

---

## Why

* Reduces psychological resistance
* Emphasizes low effort
* Improves click-through rate

---

# 3️⃣ Micro Trigger Add (Optional but Recommended)

Add checklist under STEP 1:

```text
✔ 감정이 쉽게 흔들릴 때가 있다면
✔ 혼자 정리할 시간이 필요하다면
✔ 하루를 돌아보고 싶다면
```

---

## Purpose

* Help user identify themselves
* Increase onboarding engagement

---

# 4️⃣ Tone Adjustment Rule

Apply globally to onboarding:

## Replace

* Abstract emotional phrases

## With

* Situation-based language
* Action-result clarity

---

## Example

❌ "당신은 소중합니다"
⭕ "지금 나를 돌아볼 시간이 필요하다면"

---

# 5️⃣ Success Criteria

After applying changes:

* Reduced onboarding close rate
* Increased "Next" click rate
* Increased "Start challenge" rate

---

# 🧠 Product Principle Update

Onboarding is NOT:

* Emotional comfort content

Onboarding IS:

* Self-recognition trigger + action gateway

---

자기 자신을 발견할 때 움직인다"
